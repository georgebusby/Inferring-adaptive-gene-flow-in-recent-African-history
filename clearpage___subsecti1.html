Patterns of ancestry deviation across populations

To explore ancestry deviations across the full dataset, we generated a table of the top 159 signals across all populations. These 159 signals were all above a conservative significance threshold of \(-log_{10}P\geq 8\) [Fig. \ref{fig:figSharedAncestry}; Supplementary Table 1], although we note again that identifying the correct threshold is difficult in this instance.
